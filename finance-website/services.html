Our Financial Instruments

Since 2012, we have been a trusted provider of Bank Guarantees (BG) and Standby Letters of Credit (SBLC), empowering businesses to achieve their financial goals.

Bank Guarantees (BG)

A Bank Guarantee (BG) is a promise from a lending institution that if a borrower defaults, the bank will cover the loss. This instrument enhances credibility and secures performance.

Performance Bonds: Secure contracts and projects against non-performance.

Financial Guarantees: Assure payment obligations are met between parties.

Bid Bonds: Provide assurance to project owners in competitive bidding.

Standby Letters of Credit (SBLC)

An SBLC serves as a backup payment method. It guarantees that if the primary method fails, the bank will fulfill the payment, making it a cornerstone for secure trade and finance.

Trade Finance: Facilitates secure international and domestic trade transactions.

Project Finance: Provides financial security for long-term, capital-intensive projects.

Credit Enhancement: Enhances creditworthiness for obtaining loans or financing.

Bank Guarantee (BG)

Purpose
Guarantees performance of a contractual obligation.
Usage
Construction, supply contracts, tenders.
Payment Trigger
Breach of contract or non-performance.
Risk Mitigation
Protects beneficiary against loss from non-performance.

Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC)

Purpose
Acts as a secondary payment method if the primary one fails.
Usage
International trade, financial transactions.
Payment Trigger
Non-payment or failure to meet specified conditions.
Risk Mitigation
Protects beneficiary against non-payment.
