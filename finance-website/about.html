About Finance Company Limited

Building trust and enabling global trade through reliable financial instruments since 2012.

Our Story & Mission

Established in 2012, FINANCE COMPANY LIMITED was founded with a clear vision: to demystify and simplify access to essential financial instruments like Bank Guarantees (BG) and Standby Letters of Credit (SBLC). We saw a gap in the market for a provider that was not only an expert but also a true partner to its clients.

Our mission is to empower businesses of all sizes to participate in global trade and secure large-scale projects with confidence. We achieve this by providing transparent, efficient, and reliable financial solutions tailored to the unique needs of each client.
